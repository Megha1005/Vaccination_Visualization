My Work

In this project, I will focus on trying to understand the covid-19 vaccination trends in different countries by looking at visualizations of different parameters using python libraries such as Matplotlib / Seaborn / Plotly. In particular, I am in understanding the following through data visualization:
  • What is the progress of vaccination in different countries
  • Find top countries leading in the vaccination program
  • Find countries that are lagging in the vaccination program
